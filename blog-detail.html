  • 4 LESSONS IN STYLING FROM MADEWELL'S FALL LOOKBOOK
    A well-done lookbook can be a veritable treasure trove of styling tricks. Madewell's Fall offering has some stellar standout pieces that—almost—make us want to get on with the summer already. In the meantime, we're taking notes on some creative ways to wear the pieces.

    1. Pair Your All-In-One with Clean White Sneaks: The tan color of this flight suit is spot-on and we love the sporty vibe of wearing it with trainers.

    2. Single Sole Pumps Elevate Cropped Trousers: The Palazzo pant is making a triumphant return, and looks modern with a classic heel.

    3. Leather Midi + Brogues + Socks: The midi skirt has become a staple—freshen up the look with a school girl take on footwear.

    4. Necklace Over Knit: The 90s vibe of a chunky sweater with light wash boyfriend jeans and some plaid waist accoutrement gets a feminine wink care of a wrapped, thin gold chain.

    Fashion 12 Jun 2014 Hamad Hassan
